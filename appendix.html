Appendix

PV - Peak to Valley - the difference in height from the highest peak to the lowest valley. Generally measured in fraction of waves. See waves below.

wave (general)- Generally defined to be green at 550 nm, but often a HeNe (Helium Neon) laser is used for this test and is at 632.8 nm.

f/# - The focal ratio, or "f number" as it is commonly called, is the ratio of the focal length to the diameter of the mirror. For example, a telescope with an aperture (diameter) of 10 inches and a focal length of 80 inches, would have an f/# of 8, and be called an f/8 10-inch.

sagitta - By definition it is the depth of a circular arc, and in our case is the depth of the center of the mirror. It is often measured by placing a straight edge along the mirror and sticking something with a known distance in between that straight edge and the center of the mirror. For my sagitta needs I used a feeler gauge purchased from harbor freight..
